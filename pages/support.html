Troubleshooting

Fix common Smart Study Schedule Builder issues quickly

Sync indicator keeps spinning

Refresh the page once. If it persists, sign out and sign in again to reset session sync.

Online Typical fix: under 1 min

Card actions not updating

Check that the selected tab is active, then click Start Now or Mark Complete again.

Online State refresh may be needed

Filter hides everything

Open Filter and re-enable at least one course and one status to show matching cards.

Common Resolved in 2 clicks
